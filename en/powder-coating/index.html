Powder Coating Services – Lithuania

Professional metal surface powder coating

Powder coating

Powder coating is a modern method of finishing metal surfaces, delivering the highest level of protection and aesthetics. The process involves electrostatically applying powder paint to the metal surface, which is then cured in a specialised oven.

  • High mechanical resistance
  • Corrosion and weather resistance
  • Long-lasting colour retention
  • Eco-friendly – zero volatile organic compounds
  • Uniform coating thickness
  • Wide colour selection

Surface preparation

Quality surface preparation is the most important factor in ensuring long-lasting paint adhesion to the substrate.

  • Sandblasting
  • Chemical cleaning and degreasing
  • Priming

Colours & finishes

We offer a wide range of colours and finishes to meet any customer requirement – from standard RAL and NCS colours to special textures and effects.

  • RAL and NCS colour systems
  • Custom-order paints
  • Textures: orange peel, sand, hammer, crocodile
  • Gloss levels: ultra-matte, matte, semi-gloss, gloss
  • Anti-graffiti coatings
  • Metallic and pearlescent effects
