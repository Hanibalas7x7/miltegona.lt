9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to collect visitor statistics. Before loading analytical cookies, we ask for your consent via the banner displayed at the bottom of the page.

Cookies used:

  • Analytical cookies (Google Analytics): _ga, _ga_* — collect anonymous information about website visits (pages, session duration). Loaded only with your consent.
  • Functional (localStorage): your cookie consent choice, login session in the employee system — necessary for website operation, do not store personal data.

How to manage cookies:

  • You can decline by clicking "Decline" in the banner
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (browser settings → Privacy → Clear data)
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings
